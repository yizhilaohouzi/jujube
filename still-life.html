    Still Life

    Jujube February 23, 2025

    time has acquired a stillness,
    the hour breathes
    over a wine jug,
    it’s late, the last blows have been traded,
    a clinch and a hang on the ropes
    before the bell—I give the world
    to anyone who wants it, let them be happy:

    — Gottfried Benn
