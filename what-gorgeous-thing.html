    What Gorgeous Thing

    Jujube May 24, 2026

    I do not know what gorgeous thing
    The bluebird keeps saying,
    His voice easing out of his throat,
    beak, body into the pink air
    of the early morning.  I like it
    whatever it is. 

    — Mary Oliver
