A Winter Tree

Jujube March 15, 2026

Tangled vines,
like frayed harp-strings,
enact a wayward joy.
Worldlings have turned away,
hidden indoors to nurse their fire.
But the trunk bends 
into a question mark
between frost and rust,
challenging all the assumptions of the season. 

–Jujube
