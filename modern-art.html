    Jujube May 10, 2026

    What a splendid anagram the evening makes:
    A cubist Babel of ochre and cerulean,
    bleached by the moonlight,
    sentineled by the raptor,
    and anchored by two tiny souls, 
    like pawns on a chessboard.

    — Jujube
