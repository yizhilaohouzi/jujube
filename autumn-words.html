Autumn Words

Jujube February 1, 2026

晴空一鹤排云上，
便引诗情到碧霄。

A crane soars above the clouds and soars high,
Lifts my poetic heart up to the azure sky.

— Liu Yuxi 刘禹锡 (772-842)
